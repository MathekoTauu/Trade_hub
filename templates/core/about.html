Our Story

Trade-Hub was founded in 2025 with a simple vision: to create a platform where small and medium businesses could compete on a global scale while providing customers with access to unique, high-quality products.

Starting as a small team of e-commerce enthusiasts, we recognized the need for a marketplace that prioritizes both vendor success and customer satisfaction. Today, we're proud to serve thousands of vendors and customers worldwide.

Our commitment to excellence drives everything we do, from our rigorous vendor verification process to our 24/7 customer support team.
